Digital Stress

"Digital overload is draining our focus, sleep and peace of mind."

What's digital stress?

Digital stress refers to the metnal and emotional strain caused by the overuse of digital technology, such as: smartphones, emails, and other online platforms

Why it matters?

Digital stress can cause axiety, headaches, trouble sleeping, and difficulty focusing. It often leads to low self-esteem, social withdrawal, and fear of missing out (FOMO) from constant social media use. Over time, it can harm both mental health and real-life relationships.

Whom does it affect?

Digital stress affects students, workers and anyone who feels pressured to stay constantly connected online. It is common among people who use devices frequently for school, work or social interaction.

How can we fix it?

There are various solutions you can try including:

  1. Check messages only at certain times — This stops you from getting distracted all day.
  2. Take breaks from screens — Rest your eyes and mind by doing something else for a while.
  3. Use screen time limits — These Settings help you use your phone or apps less.
